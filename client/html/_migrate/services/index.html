Sander Design offers design solutions for gardens of all styles and sizes, from relaxed country to sophisticated urban; from estate to pocket garden, we will meet the clients’ needs. Sander Design works with a variety of artists and other craftsperson's to create original and unique environments for a broad clientele.

Each Sander Design project is as unique and individual as our clients. In order to create their personal haven, we establish a close interaction with our clientele. Consultations for design inspiration help us to envision the project through our client’s eyes which allows us to meet their needs.

We emphasize on the design of outdoor environments or any environments that are nature inspired. Our area of expertise ranges from residential yards and court yards, urban garden and town houses to terraces, roof top gardens, balconies and interior garden rooms. Zen and meditation gardens, sacred spaces and healing gardens are our distinctive niche. Nevertheless our repertoire includes many other styles be it country estates and cottage gardens; condominium courtyard gardens and temporary garden installations for special events from heritage and urban cottage gardens to contemporary design as well as Asian inspired gardens, formal or informal, organic or geometric.
